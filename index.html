The Artisans of Audio

"Captain" Jack Sterling

Lead Vocals & Keytar

A voice like velvet and a blazer like fresh snow. The undisputed frontman and stylistic anchor. With a voice as smooth as a glass-calm harbor at sunset, Jack commands the stage in his signature tailored white blazer. He handles the band's iconic synth lines on a keytar he claims was once owned by a member of Toto.

Brody "The Breeze" Fairfield

Lead Guitar

Master of the 12-string acoustic and architect of those airy, chorus-drenched guitar solos. Brody expertly blends technical skill with melodic ease, crafting riffs that feel like a cool ocean breeze. His laid-back demeanor and perfectly feathered hair make him a fan favorite.

Chase "The Rhythm" Montrose

Drums

The impeccably groomed engine room. Chase keeps the pulse steady, driving sophisticated grooves with polished precision. Known for his crisp drumming and crispier attire, he believes the only thing tighter than his fills is his collection of vintage silk shirts.

Tanner "The Titan" Reed

Bass Guitar

Tanner provides the deep, resonant groove that anchors every track. If his signature thick mustache and gold chain don't impress you, his unwavering sense of rhythm certainly will. He's the foundation that allows the others to sail smoothly.

Vance "The Velvet" Yates

Percussion & Backing Vocals

A true journeyman of the scene. Vance possesses a vibe so smooth it has its own zip code. Legend has it he has played session gigs for half the billboard charts in 1982, and his appearance here adds an extra layer of authentic yacht-rock soul.
